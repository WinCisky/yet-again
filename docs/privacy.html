Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Data Controller

The Yet Again application is maintained by WinCisky. For any request regarding your data you can contact us through the project's GitHub repository.

Data Collected

When you enable push notifications, the browser generates a push subscription that contains:

  • Endpoint — a unique URL provided by the browser's push service, used to send you notifications.
  • Encryption keys (p256dh and auth) — required to encrypt the notification content so that only your browser can read it.

We do not collect cookies, IP addresses, browsing data, or any other personal information.

Purpose of Data Processing

Push subscription data is used exclusively to send you push notifications related to the events scheduled in the application. It is not used for profiling, analytics, advertising, nor shared with third parties.

Legal Basis

Data processing is based on the explicit consent of the user, given through the notification permission requested by the browser and the voluntary activation via the "Enable Notifications" button.

Data Retention

Push subscription data is stored on the server until the user decides to unsubscribe. Upon unsubscription, all data related to the subscription is permanently deleted from the server.

How to Unsubscribe and Delete Your Data

You can revoke your consent and delete your data at any time by pressing the "Unsubscribe" button on the application's main page. This action:

  1. Removes the push subscription from your browser.
  2. Deletes all data associated with your subscription from the server.

Security

Communications between the browser and the server are made over an encrypted HTTPS connection. Push notification content is end-to-end encrypted according to the Web Push standard.

User Rights

Under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR), you have the right to access your data, rectify it, delete it, restrict its processing, or object to it. You can exercise these rights by contacting us through the project's repository or simply by unsubscribing from the application.
